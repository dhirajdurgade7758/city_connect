💰 Earn EcoCoins by Reporting Issues!

Your civic contributions are rewarded! Here's how you can earn EcoCoins by posting issues:

  • Initial Post: While there's no direct EcoCoin reward for just posting an issue (as the primary goal is resolution), your post becomes an opportunity to earn!
  • Likes: When other citizens like your issue post, you earn 1 EcoCoin per like!
  • Comments: When other citizens comment on your issue post, you earn 2 EcoCoins per comment!
  • Resolution: Once your issue is marked as 'Resolved' by the relevant department, you might receive a bonus EcoCoin reward (feature coming soon!).

The more impactful and engaging your issue posts are, the more EcoCoins you can earn. Keep an eye on your EcoCoin balance!
